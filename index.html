About

We're changing the landscape of recruiting one day at a time.

  • 2008

    Our Humble Beginnings

    With a dollar and a dream, Matt Alling leaves the corporate world to start Calvin Group.

  • 2010

    Rapid Growth

    Calvin Group is named to Inc. Magazine's The Inc. 500 (the 500 fastest growing companies in America).
    170th fastest growing in the US.
    12th fastest growing in the Staffing Industry.
    14th fastest growing in Los Angeles.

  • 2012

    First Acquisition

    Calvin Group acquires Arsenal Recruiting, an agency exclusively focused on Bay Area staffing, to expand its reach to the Greater San Francisco area.

  • 2014

    The Candidate Agency

    We realized that unlike in the world of sports or film/television where the best talent has an agent that exclusively represents them, in tech there was no such agency. There is now.
